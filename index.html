    beeHive system [2019]

    Balcony automation [2016]

    A controller for two balcony lamps and for plants watering valve. Based on a Microchip PIC Microcontroller and on a Maxim DS1307 Real Time Clock (RTC).
    It includes buttons for enabling/disabling the automated operation of the lamps and automatic/continuous plant watering, all with LED indicators. Options and settings are saved to the EEPROM of DS1307, which is battery-protected.
    The control of the lamps is based on real time, the ambient brightness and the signal of a PIR movement sensor.
    Watering system depends on day/time, as well as on the season of the year.

    Tasks Timer [2014]

    A Microchip PIC16F887 & DS1307 based system measuring elapsed time for up to ten different tasks. It features alarm, chimes, and a countdown timer, all controlled from a menu.
    The circuit is designed in a "sandwich" format, allowing easy upgrade or modules change. Battery layer can be added, which features a LiPo battery with charger and voltage regulator.
    When system operates on external power, brown-out & black-out events are detected and trigger data saving in EEPROM. Data can load automatically on startup (continue where it left).

    Adjustable symmetrical Power Supply Unit, with voltage and current indication.

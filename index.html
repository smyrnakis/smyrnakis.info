beeHive system [2019]

A remote beehive surveillance system, monitoring honey production (weight) and ambient environment (temperature & humidity).
The system can connect to the Internet using WiFi or GPRS. The data is uploaded to ThingSpeak IoT platform and can also be sent to the apiarist over an SMS.

In collaboration with @nickap.

Balcony automation [2016]

A controller for two balcony lamps and for plants watering valve. Based on a Microchip PIC Microcontroller and on a Maxim DS1307 Real Time Clock (RTC).
It includes buttons for enabling/disabling the automated operation of the lamps and automatic/continuous plant watering, all with LED indicators. Options and settings are saved to the DS1307 EEPROM and are battery-protected.
The control of the lamps is based on real time, the ambient brightness and the signal of a PIR movement sensor. Tasks Timer [2014]

A Microchip PIC16F887 & DS1307 based system measuring elapsed time for up to ten different tasks. It features alarm, chimes, and a countdown timer. Brown-out & black-out detection and automatic data saving in EEPROM.
